About Me

Rajeshwar Dayal is Asst. Professor of Computer Science & Applications at International School of Management, Patna. He is approved academic counsellor of Indira Gandhi National Open University(IGNOU) and also an adjunct faculty of Sikkim Manipal University. Appart from this he is visiting faculty of IIBM-ZHI group of Instutions. Before joining the teaching, he has worked in State Election Comission, Bihar as EDP I/c and prior to that was associated with Water & Power Consultancy Services(WAPCOS(I) Ltd.), New Delhi as Jr.Consultant (Computer Applications).
